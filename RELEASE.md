# Release Notes: Jeeves v0.9.2

We are thrilled to announce **Jeeves v0.9.2**, featuring a brand-new App Icon, a comprehensive UI overhaul, and the highly anticipated **Jotter** module!

## Highlights

### 👔 New Brand & Icon
- **Jeeves Identity:** The app has officially been rebranded to "Jeeves".
- **New App Icon:** Replaced the legacy Hermes icon with a sleek new Butler logo. It adapts seamlessly to all devices using Android Adaptive Icons.

### 🎨 "Sassy Butler" Dark Theme
- Transitioned the entire app to the "Butler Theme"—a dark, sophisticated aesthetic.
- Enhanced contrast and typography to give the app a premium feel.
- Optimized layouts across chat and settings.

### 📝 Jotter (Notes Module)
- **Notesnook-inspired UI:** A brand new, ultra-clean markdown editor and sidebar.
- **Distraction-Free Editor:** A minimal layout focusing on your content, featuring a massive, bold title input and a simplified bottom toolbar for formatting (`+`, **B**, *I*, `U`, etc.).
- **Smart Sidebar:** A newly designed drawer containing exactly what you need—Notes, Favorites, Reminders, Monographs, Archive, and Trash.

## Upgrading
Just download the `app-universal-release.apk` and install it on your device. Existing data will be preserved.

## Technical Changes
- Updated Android resource generation (`ic_launcher`) to incorporate the new logo.
- Migrated away from deprecated Material Icons to `AutoMirrored` vectors.
- Overhauled Jetpack Compose layouts in `NoteApp.kt` to refine the Jotter UI.
